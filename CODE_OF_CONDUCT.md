# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

Prism Bits is dedicated to providing a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for everyone, regardless of background or identity. We as contributors and maintainers pledge to uphold this Code of Conduct in all community spaces.

## Our Standards

We expect all community members—contributors, users, maintainers, and others—to:

- Treat others with respect and kindness.
- Be considerate of differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Acknowledge and credit others’ work appropriately.
- Refrain from harassment, intimidation, or discrimination.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes (but is not limited to):

- Harassment, bullying, stalking, or intimidation.
- Discriminatory behavior or hate speech of any kind.
- Offensive or demeaning language targeting a person or group.
- Sexual content or advances in community spaces.
- Threats of violence or other harmful conduct.
- Disruption of discussions or deliberate bad-faith actions.
- Publishing others’ private personal information (doxxing) without explicit consent.
- Any other conduct that the maintainers reasonably consider inappropriate in a community context.

## Reporting a Concern

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it as soon as possible.

- Email: `saisrujanpunati@gmail.com`
- Include: what happened, where/when it happened, and any relevant links or screenshots.

Reports will be reviewed by the maintainers or moderators. If you are not comfortable reporting by email, contact the maintainers directly at `saisrujanpunati@gmail.com`.

## Enforcement

Maintainers/moderators may take any action they deem appropriate in response to unacceptable behavior, including:

- Warning the offender
- Temporarily or permanently removing access or contributions
- Removing content that violates this Code of Conduct
- Banning repeat offenders

Maintainers/moderators will aim to be fair, consistent, and respectful.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all community spaces associated with Prism Bits, including:

- GitHub repositories and issues
- Pull requests
- Discussions
- Documentation and project communications

## Attribution

This Code of Conduct is based on commonly used community standards (similar in spirit to the Contributor Covenant format). You can update the reporting contact details to match your project’s needs.
